Scramble

How to Play

  • Slide & Swap: Slide a character into an open tile. But, whenever you slide a character, its two neighbors swap places too!
  • Pac-Man Doors: Moving off the right and left edges wraps characters completely around to the opposite edge.
  • The Jewel Track: The eye icon is normally closed. Tap the Eye icon to view the optimal pathway.
