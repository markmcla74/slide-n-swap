Scramble

How to Play

  • Slide & Swap: Slide characters to empty spots until all characters are home. BUT, when you move a character, its two neighbors swap places.
  • Pac-Man Doors: Moving off the left and right screen edges wraps characters completely around to the opposite edge instantly!
  • The Jewel Track: Puzzles launch blind. Solve from memory or tap the Eye icon to view the optimal pathway.
