How to Play Slide 'n Swap

  • Slide & Swap: Shift tokens along grid vertices to match their designated home slots.
  • Pac-Man Doors: Moving off screen edges wraps characters completely around to the opposite edge instantly!
  • The Jewel Track: Puzzles launch blind. Solve from memory or tap the Eye icon to view the optimal pathway.
